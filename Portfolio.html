About Me

I'm Faiza—a Computer Science student with a quiet fire and a mind wired for creation. I’m currently focused on web development and database management, building not just projects but digital experiences that make sense, feel right, and actually work. I'm doing my six-month internship where I’m applying what I’ve learned, pushing past theory into real-world solutions. I’m the kind of person who can lose track of time solving a bug or perfecting a layout, but I never forget the why behind what I build. Clean design, functional structure, seamless flow—that’s what I aim for. Whether I’m organizing data, structuring content, or crafting interfaces, I bring a mix of logic and creative intuition to the table. I’ve worn different hats—virtual assistant, content creator, designer, and digital organizer—especially while helping with my mom’s catering business. That experience taught me discipline, time management, and how to handle pressure with grace. It also sharpened my skills in tools like Canva, Microsoft Office, and Google Workspace. But I’m not just what I do. I’m also a Pisces—yes, I feel deeply and dream wildly. I thrive in quiet spaces, but my ambitions are loud. I’m drawn to dark romance stories and K-dramas because I love complexity, layered truths, and the beauty in imperfection. My favorite color? Black. Because it’s bold, timeless, and unapologetic—just like the energy I bring to my work. Right now, I’m on a path of growth. I dedicate time every day to learning, challenging myself, and improving my craft. I don’t want to just be another name in tech—I want to leave a mark, build meaningful things, and evolve into someone even my future self would admire.
